// about

Profile

I'm an RTL Design Engineer working across the ASIC and FPGA sides of RISC‑V based SoC development — from block-level SystemVerilog and sign-off, up through chip-level firmware bring-up on silicon and FPGA hardware.

My day-to-day spans high-speed I/O (32 Gbps SerDes, PCIe Gen5), protocol bridges and CDC-safe interconnect, low-power design, and JTAG/debug infrastructure — alongside longer-running research into out-of-order CPU microarchitecture and cache-coherent multicore systems.

I hold the RISC‑V Foundational Associate (RVFA) certification, contributed to Google Summer of Code in 2022 and mentored in 2023 on the Azadi‑SoC project, and I'm currently expanding my open-source RV32I core with out-of-order execution and exploring CHI-based cache coherency.

Open to ASIC / FPGA / RISC‑V design roles — Pakistan, Europe, UAE, and beyond.
